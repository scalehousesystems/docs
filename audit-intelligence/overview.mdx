## What is Audit Intelligence?

Audit Intelligence is ScaleHouse's core feature that continuously monitors your practice management system audit logs to detect compliance gaps and security violations in real-time.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-Time Monitoring" icon="activity">
    Audit logs synced every 2 minutes from your PMS
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gap Detection" icon="shield">
    Automated detection of compliance violations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Scoring" icon="chart-line">
    Track your compliance posture over time
  </Card>
  <Card title="Alerts" icon="bell">
    Instant notifications for critical issues
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Install and activate a connector for your PMS (Open Dental, Dentrix, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync">
    The connector syncs audit logs to ScaleHouse every 2 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze">
    Gap detection algorithms analyze events every 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Alert">
    Critical issues trigger immediate alerts in your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
