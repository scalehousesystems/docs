## What is ScaleHouse Systems?

ScaleHouse Systems is the **first AI-powered compliance intelligence platform** for healthcare. We automate HIPAA and OSHA compliance by connecting to your practice management systems, continuously monitoring audit logs, detecting compliance gaps, and auto-generating compliance documents.

**Replace $5-15K/year compliance consultants with software.**

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Real-Time Audit Intelligence">
    Connect to Open Dental, Dentrix, or Gusto and automatically collect audit logs every 2 minutes. Detect compliance gaps in real-time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cross-System Gap Detection">
    Correlate data across PMS and payroll systems to detect critical gaps like terminated employees with active access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Document Generation">
    Generate Security Risk Assessments, Audit Log Summaries, and other compliance documents automatically via AI.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Multi-Location Support">
    Manage compliance across multiple practice locations from a single dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Connector">
    Download and install the Windows connector on your office computer. Takes 5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate">
    Generate an activation token in the dashboard and paste it into the connector. Connects to your PMS automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor">
    The connector sends audit logs to the cloud every 2 minutes. Gap detection runs automatically every 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remediate">
    View alerts in the dashboard, get AI-powered remediation steps, and generate compliance documents on demand.
  </Step>
</Steps>
