## Overview

Use AI to automatically generate compliance documents like Security Risk Assessments, policies, and audit summaries.

  <Card title="Incident Reports" icon="alert-circle">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Generating Documents

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Document Type">
    Choose the type of document you want to generate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Context">
    Enter relevant information or select data sources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **"Generate"** and wait for AI processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    Review the generated document and make edits if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download as PDF or Word document.
  </Step>
</Steps>
