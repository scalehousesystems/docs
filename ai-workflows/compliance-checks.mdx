## Overview

Automated compliance checks verify your organization's compliance with specific requirements.

## Running Checks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Framework">
    Choose HIPAA or OSHA framework.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Requirements">
    Choose specific requirements to check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Check">
    Click **"Run Compliance Check"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results">
    View pass/fail status for each requirement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check Results

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pass" icon="check-circle">
    Requirement is met
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fail" icon="x-circle">
    Requirement needs attention
  </Card>
  <Card title="Warning" icon="alert-triangle">
    Potential issue detected
  </Card>
  <Card title="Not Applicable" icon="minus">
    Requirement doesn't apply
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
