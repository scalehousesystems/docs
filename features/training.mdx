## Assigning Training

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Module">
    Choose a training module from the training page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign">
    Select team members to assign the training to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Deadline">
    Optionally set a completion deadline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **"Assign Training"**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
