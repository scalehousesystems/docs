## Overview

Team management allows you to invite team members, assign roles, and manage permissions.

## Roles

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Full access to all features
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="user-check">
    Administrative access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="user">
    Limited access based on permissions
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team">
    Navigate to **Team** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite">
    Click **"Invite Member"** and enter their email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Select the appropriate role for the member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **"Send Invitation"**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions

Permissions are role-based and control access to:
- Dashboard data
- Framework management
- Program creation
- Evidence upload
- Team management
