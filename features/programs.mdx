## Overview

Programs help you organize your compliance activities, including risk assessments, procedures, and policies.

## Creating Programs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Programs">
    Go to **Programs** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click **"Create Program"** and select the program type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Fill in program details and requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save and start tracking your program.
  </Step>
</Steps>
