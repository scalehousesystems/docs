## Overview

The AI Assistant helps you generate compliance documents, answer questions, and automate compliance tasks.

## Using the AI Assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assistant">
    Click the **AI Assistant** button in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask Question">
    Type your question or request in the chat interface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Response">
    Review the AI-generated response and recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Action">
    Follow the suggested steps or generate documents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Document Generation

The AI Assistant can generate:
- Security Risk Assessments (SRA)
- Audit log summaries
- Compliance policies
- Incident reports
- Training materials
