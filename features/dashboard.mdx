## Overview

The ScaleHouse dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your compliance posture, alerts, and audit intelligence.

## Key Metrics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Compliance Score" icon="chart-line">
    Your overall compliance score (0-100)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Gaps" icon="alert-triangle">
    Number of unresolved compliance gaps
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connector Status" icon="plug">
    Status of your connected systems
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recent Events" icon="activity">
    Latest audit log events
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Navigation

The dashboard includes sections for:
- **Frameworks:** HIPAA and OSHA compliance tracking
- **Programs:** Risk assessments and procedures
- **Remediation:** Task board for addressing gaps
- **Evidence:** Document repository
- **Team:** People management
- **Training:** Compliance training modules
- **AI Assistant:** AI-powered workflows
