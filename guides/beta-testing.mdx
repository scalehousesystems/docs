## Overview

Thank you for participating in ScaleHouse beta testing! This guide will help you get the most out of the beta program.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your beta account at [scalehousesystems.com/signup](https://scalehousesystems.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Follow the onboarding wizard to set up your organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Connector">
    Download and install the connector (see [Installation Guide](/docs/installation)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Testing">
    Begin using ScaleHouse and provide feedback!
  </Step>
</Steps>
