## Overview

ScaleHouse supports managing compliance across multiple practice locations from a single dashboard.

## Setting Up Locations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Location">
    Go to **Settings â†’ Locations** and click **"Add Location"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide location name, address, and contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Connector">
    Link a connector to this location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set location-specific compliance settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
